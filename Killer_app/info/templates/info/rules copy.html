Rules !

Killer Game

Description:

Killer is a famous role-playing game.
The aim is to be the last player still alive and kill as many players as possible.
This application lets you organize a Killer with as many players as you like.
This game can be played between friends, colleagues, leisure center organizers, etc.
Once the game has started, you need to be on your guard 24 hours a day.

Features:

1. The game master fill out form for each player:
     - pseudo
     - e-mail address
     - custom action (optional)
He/she also can propose Kill examples that will be randomly assigned to players.
NB: The custom action is written in the sense that the player has to do it,
        NOT in the sense that he has to do it to be killed.
Example: the custom action for Alex is 'make him wear a hat'. The game will assign him a target.
He will have to make his target wear a hat.

2. Finally, each player will receive a mission order by Email:
     -A target
     -A Kill

Once each player has received his mission order, the game can begin!

Attention:

This game is purely a life-size role-playing game, and in no way alludes to any notion of violence, degradation, or other negative facts.
